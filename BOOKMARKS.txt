The following list is some of the most common bookmarking sites you can add. Just copy and paste to the Social Bookmarking page of the Admin CP.
The icons are already included in the images/bookmarks folder:

Digg
http://digg.com/submit?phrase=2&url={url}&title={title}
images/bookmarks/digg.gif

del.icio.us
http://del.icio.us/post?url={url}&title={title}
images/bookmarks/delicious.gif

StumbleUpon
http://www.stumbleupon.com/submit?url={url}&title={title}
images/bookmarks/stumbleupon.gif

Facebook
http://www.facebook.com/sharer.php?u={url}&title={title}
images/bookmarks/facebook.gif

Google
http://www.google.com/bookmarks/mark?op=edit&bkmk={url}&title={title}
images/bookmarks/google.gif

MySpace
http://www.myspace.com/Modules/PostTo/Pages/?t={title}&c=&u={url}&l=2
images/bookmarks/myspace.gif

Furl
http://www.furl.net/storeIt.jsp?u={url}&amp;t={title}
images/bookmarks/furl.gif

Reddit
http://reddit.com/submit?url={url}&amp;title={title}
images/bookmarks/reddit.gif

Live
https://favorites.live.com/quickadd.aspx?marklet=1&mkt=en-us&url={url}&title={title}
images/bookmarks/live.gif

Yahoo
http://myweb2.search.yahoo.com/myresults/bookmarklet?t={title}&u={url}
images/bookmarks/yahoo.gif

Twitter
http://twitter.com/?status={title} {url}
images/bookmarks/twitter.gif

Bebo
http://www.bebo.com/c/share?Url={url}&Title={title}
images/bookmarks/bebo.gif

LinkedIn
http://www.linkedin.com/shareArticle?url={url}&title={title}
images/bookmarks/linkedin.png

Technorati
http://technorati.com/faves?add={url}
images/bookmarks/technorati.png

Blogger
http://www.blogger.com/blog-this.g?u={url}&n={title}
images/bookmarks/blogger.png

Fark
http://www.fark.com/cgi/farkit.pl?u={url}
